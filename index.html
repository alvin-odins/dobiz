DoBiz Africa

Dobiz Africa is premiere project designed to among other things encourage entrepreneurship among young people by rendering country specific information on the various requirements, laws and guidelines that will enable one engage in successful enterprise. Dobiz Africa also seeks to connect African Businesses to the world as well as promote intra Africa trade.
